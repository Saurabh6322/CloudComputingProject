Green Salad

Healthy Vegetarian Prep time: 15 mins | Cook time: 0 mins

Ingredients

  • 4 cups mixed greens (spinach, arugula, lettuce)
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, diced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup toasted nuts (walnuts or almonds)
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese (optional)
  • For the dressing:
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the vegetables: Wash and dry all vegetables thoroughly. Slice, dice, or julienne as indicated in the ingredients list.
  2. Make the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, Dijon mustard, minced garlic, salt, pepper, and honey (if using) until well combined.
  3. Assemble the salad: In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, cucumber, bell pepper, carrot, red onion, and cherry tomatoes.
  4. Add toppings: Sprinkle the toasted nuts and crumbled feta cheese (if using) over the salad.
  5. Dress the salad: Just before serving, drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat all ingredients evenly.
  6. Serve: Divide the salad among plates and serve immediately for maximum freshness.

Tips

  • For a protein boost, add grilled chicken, hard-boiled eggs, or chickpeas.
  • Only dress the portion of salad you plan to eat immediately. Store leftover salad and dressing separately.
  • For a vegan version, omit the feta cheese or substitute with a plant-based alternative.
  • Add seasonal fruits like apple slices or berries for a sweet contrast.
