Masala Dosa

Indian Breakfast Prep time: 8 hours | Cook time: 30 mins

Ingredients

  • 3 cups rice (parboiled or regular)
  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/4 teaspoon fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking
  • For potato filling:
  • 4 large potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 sprig curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the batter: Wash and soak rice and urad dal separately for 4-6 hours. Add fenugreek seeds to the urad dal while soaking.
  2. Grind the batter: Drain the water and grind urad dal to a smooth paste. Then grind rice to a slightly coarse consistency. Mix both batters together, add salt, and let it ferment overnight (8-12 hours).
  3. Prepare potato filling: Heat oil in a pan, add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add curry leaves, green chilies, and ginger. Sauté for a minute.
  4. Add onions: Add chopped onions and sauté until translucent.
  5. Add spices: Add turmeric powder and salt. Mix well.
  6. Add potatoes: Add mashed potatoes and mix thoroughly. Cook for 3-4 minutes. Add chopped coriander leaves and turn off the heat.
  7. Make dosas: Heat a non-stick or cast iron pan. Pour a ladleful of batter and spread it in a circular motion to make a thin dosa.
  8. Cook the dosa: Drizzle oil or ghee around the edges. Cook until the bottom turns golden brown.
  9. Add filling: Place a portion of the potato filling in the center of the dosa. Fold the dosa over the filling.
  10. Serve: Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.

Tips

  • The fermentation process is crucial for a good dosa batter. In colder climates, you may need to place the batter in a warm spot or use a yogurt maker.
  • The consistency of the batter should be similar to pancake batter - not too thick or too thin.
  • A well-seasoned cast iron pan works best for making crispy dosas.
