Fruit Smoothie

Healthy Breakfast Prep time: 5 mins | Cook time: 0 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 banana, frozen and sliced
  • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional, for a thicker consistency)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the fruits: If using fresh berries, wash them thoroughly. If the banana isn't already frozen, slice it and freeze for at least 2 hours for a creamier smoothie.
  2. Add ingredients to blender: Place the frozen banana slices, mixed berries, Greek yogurt, and milk in a blender.
  3. Add optional ingredients: If desired, add honey or maple syrup for sweetness, chia seeds for extra nutrition, and vanilla extract for flavor.
  4. Blend: Blend on high speed until smooth and creamy, about 30-60 seconds. If the mixture is too thick, add more milk. If it's too thin, add more frozen fruit or ice cubes.
  5. Serve: Pour into glasses and serve immediately. Garnish with additional berries or a sprinkle of chia seeds if desired.

Tips

  • For a dairy-free version, use almond milk, oat milk, or coconut milk.
  • Add a handful of spinach or kale for a nutrient boost without significantly changing the flavor.
  • For a protein-rich smoothie, add a scoop of protein powder or a tablespoon of nut butter.
  • Freeze leftover smoothie in popsicle molds for a healthy frozen treat.
