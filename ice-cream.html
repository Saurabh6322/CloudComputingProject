Homemade Ice Cream

Dessert Frozen Prep time: 20 mins | Freeze time: 4 hours

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • Optional mix-ins: chocolate chips, crushed cookies, fruit, nuts

Instructions

  1. Prepare the base: In a large bowl, whisk together the sugar and salt with the milk until the sugar is dissolved.
  2. Add cream and vanilla: Stir in the heavy cream and vanilla extract until well combined.
  3. Chill the mixture: Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or overnight if possible. The colder the mixture, the better it will freeze.
  4. Churn the ice cream: Pour the chilled mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions, usually 20-25 minutes or until it reaches a soft-serve consistency.
  5. Add mix-ins: In the last few minutes of churning, add any desired mix-ins like chocolate chips, crushed cookies, or fruit.
  6. Freeze until firm: Transfer the ice cream to an airtight container. Press a piece of parchment paper directly on the surface of the ice cream to prevent ice crystals from forming. Seal the container and freeze for at least 4 hours or until firm.
  7. Serve: Let the ice cream sit at room temperature for 5 minutes before scooping to soften slightly for the perfect texture.

Flavor Variations

  • Chocolate: Add 1/4 cup cocoa powder with the sugar.
  • Strawberry: Fold in 1 cup of macerated strawberries after churning.
  • Mint Chocolate Chip: Add 1 teaspoon peppermint extract and fold in 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips.
  • Coffee: Add 2 tablespoons instant espresso powder with the sugar.

Tips

  • For the creamiest texture, use full-fat dairy products.
  • If you don't have an ice cream maker, pour the mixture into a shallow container and freeze, stirring vigorously every 30 minutes for about 3 hours.
  • Store ice cream in the back of the freezer where it's coldest and most consistent in temperature.
  • For softer ice cream, add 1-2 tablespoons of alcohol like vodka or rum to the mixture.
