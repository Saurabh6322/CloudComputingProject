Cheesecake

Dessert Baking Prep time: 30 mins | Cook time: 55 mins

Ingredients for Crust

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Ingredients for Filling

  • 4 (8 oz) packages cream cheese, room temperature
  • 1 1/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup sour cream, room temperature
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 5 large eggs, room temperature
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Prepare the pan: Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan and wrap the outside with aluminum foil to prevent water from seeping in during baking.
  2. Make the crust: In a medium bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, sugar, melted butter, and salt. Press the mixture firmly into the bottom of the prepared pan. Bake for 10 minutes, then remove and let cool.
  3. Make the filling: In a large bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth. Add sugar and beat until combined. Add sour cream and vanilla, beat until smooth. Add eggs one at a time, beating after each addition. Stir in flour and salt.
  4. Prepare water bath: Place the springform pan in a larger roasting pan. Pour the filling over the crust. Fill the roasting pan with hot water to reach halfway up the sides of the springform pan.
  5. Bake: Bake for 55-65 minutes, or until the edges are set but the center still jiggles slightly. Turn off the oven, crack the door open, and let the cheesecake cool in the oven for 1 hour.
  6. Chill: Remove from the water bath, run a knife around the edge of the cake, and let cool completely. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight before serving.

Tips

  • Make sure all ingredients are at room temperature for a smooth, lump-free batter.
  • Don't overmix after adding the eggs to avoid incorporating too much air.
  • The water bath helps prevent cracks by providing gentle, even heat.
  • For a fruity topping, spread 1 cup of your favorite fruit preserves over the cooled cheesecake.
