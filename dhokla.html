Dhokla

Indian Snack Prep time: 8 hours | Cook time: 20 mins

Ingredients

  • For the batter:
  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1/4 cup semolina (sooji)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-green chili paste
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon fruit salt (eno) or 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • For the tempering:
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds
  • 2-3 green chilies, slit
  • 10-12 curry leaves
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped coriander leaves
  • 2 tablespoons freshly grated coconut (optional)

Instructions

  1. Prepare the batter: In a large bowl, mix gram flour, semolina, yogurt, water, ginger-green chili paste, sugar, and salt. Whisk well to form a smooth batter without lumps. Let it ferment for 4-6 hours.
  2. Prepare the steamer: Grease a steaming plate or a cake tin. Heat water in a steamer or a large pot.
  3. Add leavening agent: Just before steaming, add fruit salt or baking soda and oil to the batter. Mix gently but thoroughly. The batter will become frothy.
  4. Steam the dhokla: Pour the batter into the greased plate. Steam for 15-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
  5. Cool and cut: Let it cool for 5 minutes. Cut into diamond or square shapes.
  6. Prepare the tempering: Heat oil in a small pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add sesame seeds, green chilies, and curry leaves. Sauté for a few seconds.
  7. Add water and lemon juice: Add water and lemon juice to the tempering. Let it simmer for a minute.
  8. Pour over dhokla: Pour the tempering evenly over the cut dhokla pieces.
  9. Garnish and serve: Garnish with chopped coriander leaves and freshly grated coconut. Serve with green chutney.

Tips

  • For a quicker version, you can use instant dhokla mix available in stores.
  • The fermentation time can be reduced by keeping the batter in a warm place.
  • Make sure the steamer is preheated before placing the dhokla batter for steaming.
  • Dhokla can be stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator for 2-3 days.
